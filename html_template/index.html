About Us

Madurai Bible School was born out of a desire for believers in Tamil Nadu to have the opportunity to pursue a solid, Biblical study without having to leave the state. Founded in 1955, MBS has faithfully trained men and women in the Word of God for over 69 years.

Through the Lord's grace, it has grown from humble beginnings to a fully established Bible College with recognized courses and hundreds of alumni serving Christ across the world.

Our History

69 years of faithful service in Biblical education and ministry training

1955

Madurai Bible School founded with a vision to provide Biblical education in Tamil Nadu.

1960

Expansion of curriculum and establishment of residential facilities for students.

1978

Introduction of Bachelor in Theology program with recognized accreditation.

1984

Establishment of ministry training programs and field experience initiatives.

2024

Celebrating 69 years of faithful service with hundreds of alumni serving Christ worldwide.

Student Life

Our Motto

"Be a Disciple and Make Disciples"

This guiding principle shapes every aspect of student life at MBC, from classroom learning to practical ministry experience.

Gurukul System of Education

We embrace the traditional Gurukul model where students live and learn in close community with their teachers. This holistic approach fosters deep relationships and character development.

Students experience not just academic instruction but life-on-life mentorship that prepares them for effective ministry.

Residential Life

Comfortable hostel facilities providing a home away from home for students during their studies.

Ministry Opportunities

Regular outreach programs, church ministry, and field experience to apply classroom learning.
